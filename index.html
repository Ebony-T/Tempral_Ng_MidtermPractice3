Why Eat Junk Food?

Benefits

  • It gives you a dopamine release.
  • It helps satisfy cravings.
  • It’s convenient and easily accessible.
  • It’s affordable.
  • It tastes really good.

Healthy food is very nourishing but it’s important that everyone indulges often because contrary to popular belief, there are many benefits to eating junk food. Specifically, it's helpful for one’s mental health. Junk food can bring a great deal of comfort to those going through a hard time or it's a great way to treat oneself when celebrating achievements. Couple this with its general convenience and affordability, junk food is the way to go!

YOU ARE WHAT YOU EAT

Why Eat Healthy Foods?

Healthy foods keeps your body in balance and on track. It helps you manage your weight as well live a healthier youthful lifestyle.

Benefits

  • It protects you against many chronic noncommunicable diseases, such as heart disease, diabetes and cancer.
  • Helps the digestive system function.
  • Helps achieve and maintain a healthy weight.
  • It may also boost the mood and gain more energy by maintaining a balanced diet.

In conclusion, Junk food may be quite convenient, readily available on the go, cheap whereas healthy food is best for maintaining weight, getting an adequate amount of essential nutrients and for keeping you in good state of health. When you consume a diet that is packed with natural fresh produce, it facilitates to lower the risk of several chronic disorders.
